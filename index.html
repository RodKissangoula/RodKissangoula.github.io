About Me

Hello, I’m Rod, a college graduate with a passion for using technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful solutions. I enjoy building applications, working with databases, and exploring digital forensics, especially when it involves breaking down complex problems and figuring out how everything connects.

My journey into information technology wasn’t something I had planned from the start. I entered college undecided and spent my first two years trying to figure out what direction I wanted to take. It wasn’t until my advisor encouraged me to explore IT, after recognizing my background from a technology high school and my experience in a tech club, that I decided to give it a chance.

At first, it wasn’t easy. Learning how to code felt overwhelming, like learning an entirely new language. There were moments where I questioned whether I had chosen the right path. What made the difference was having a professor who broke things down in a way that finally made sense, along with the time I spent outside of class teaching myself and practicing in the library. Even then, I wouldn’t say I loved it yet; I was just determined to improve and succeed.

Everything changed during a final project where I had to build a website from scratch using everything I had learned. It was challenging and frustrating at times, but solving problems and seeing my ideas come to life gave me a sense of accomplishment I hadn’t felt before. That experience made something click for me. I realized that I didn’t just understand the material; I genuinely enjoyed the process. That’s when I knew I had found the right field.

Since then, I’ve gained hands-on experience in web development, mobile app development, database management, and digital forensics investigations. I’ve worked with technologies like Java, C++, SQL, JavaScript, PHP, and HTML/CSS, and I enjoy continuing to grow my skill set. I also value collaboration and learning from others, as I believe the best solutions often come from shared ideas and perspectives.

Away from the screen, I like to stay active and take time to recharge. Sports have always been a big part of my life; I enjoy playing and watching soccer, basketball, and football. Whether it’s playing with friends or being in the crowd at a live game, I really appreciate the energy and excitement that comes with it.

I’m also a big fan of music and enjoy going to concerts whenever I get the chance. There’s something about experiencing music live that’s completely different from just listening to it. These interests help me stay balanced, clear my mind, and bring a fresh perspective when I return to working on technical problems.

I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, connect with others, and apply my skills to projects that make a positive impact.
